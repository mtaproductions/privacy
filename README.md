# privacy
TradingBook - Privacy Policy

TradingBook is designed to keep your trading data private.

TradingBook does not collect, store, sell, or share your personal trading data. Any trade entries, balances, notes, settings, and performance information you enter are stored locally on your device.

We do not operate a server for storing your trade data, and we cannot view, recover, or restore data entered into the app. If you delete the app from your device, your locally stored app data may be permanently lost.

TradingBook uses Apple’s App Store and StoreKit services to manage subscriptions and purchases. Apple may process purchase information according to Apple’s own privacy policy.

TradingBook does not use your data for advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics.

If future versions add optional cloud backup, account features, analytics, or other online services, this policy will be updated before those features are used.

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support contact listed on the App Store.
